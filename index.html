Jack Skellington

About Jack

Jack is the "Pumpkin King" of Halloween Town and the patron og Halloween. He is in charge of Halloween Town's celebration, respected and even idolized by other residents for his role. He is a hero with enerything he could ever want at his skeletal finger tips yet he yearns for more and feels alone in his wants and wishes.

Jack's characteristics

  1. Very charming, can sway all but one. His true love Sally
  2. Impulsive and at times selfish
  3. Enthusiastic and Inclusive
  4. A loyal and caring friend

Jack's friends

  • Sora, The Keyblade bearer
  • Sally, his one true love
  • Zero, his loyal undead pup
  • Doc Finklestein, his friend and inventor
